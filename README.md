A personal project exploring data in the world of esports.

Guiding Questions:
- Given a draft and a context for the relative skill of the players (including possibly on their specific champion), how likely is a certain team to win?
- How relevant is the draft to the outcome of a match? How relevant is player skill to the outcome of a match?
	- (i.e. which is more important: the draft or the relative skill of the players?)
- During the draft, what parts of team composition should be prioritized? (i.e. personal champion synergy, synergy between champions on the same team, lane counters, early vs late game, teamfight vs. pick, balancing engage/disengage)
- Is there a way to quantify how good certain coaches are at drafting (i.e. some draft win probability added statistic)?
- How does side selection play a role? Which side is actually better for a certain team in a certain scenario?
- How do meta shifts affect draft-based win probability?
